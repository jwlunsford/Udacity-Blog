American Black Walnut

Black walnut(Juglans nigra) is a deciduous hardwood tree that grows in moist bottomland soils. This tree prefers growing in sunlight but will aslo thrive in partial shade. Its wood is used to make furniture, gun stocks, and high quality veneer.

Eastern Redbud

Eastern Redbud(Cercis canadensis) is a non-commercial decidous tree that grows in moist well-drained sites on gentle slopes and along stream banks. This tree is one of the earliest flowering trees in East Texas in early March, and its bright pink flowers attract native bees.

Post Oak

Post oak(Quercus stellata) is a deciduous hardwood species, and the most common oak tree found in Texas. Post oak is a medium-sized tree, and it provides critical habitat for birds and mammals alike. Its wood is used to make cross-ties and posts.
